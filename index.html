R-Trento Users Group (RTUG)

The R-Trento Users Group is a shared space for academics, scholars, students, and professionals living and working in Trentino and surrounding areas that are passionate about the R language and its suite of tools.

Mission

RTUG mission is to spread the use or R language and tools within academia, industry and public administration.
